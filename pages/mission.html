OUR MISSION

About Divas in Technology

From this stem, a flower blooms

- DTech Official Motto

Divas in Technology (DTech) is a platform created by Tania Mishra to expand the outreach of computer science to younger girls. The goal of this platform is to host multiple computer science workshops and programs for girls of all ages, ethnicities, and backgrounds. Our mission is to help inspire girls to close the gender gap in technology-related career fields. We want to help younger girls gain resources early on by hosting workshops led by high school and college students who are intending to pursue a career in a technology field. Networking opportunities will also be included through guest speakers who are currently in the workforce.
