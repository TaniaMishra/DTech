Intro to Coding, Python

Intro to Coding, Python

Spring 2022

Led by DTech, In partnership with Journey House

Who: 7th-8th graders, identify as a girl, no programming experience required
When: May 21-22, 9am-4pm
Where: Journey House, Milwaukee
What: In this two day bootcamp, students will learn the basics of coding in Python. They will learn about algorithms, variables, conditionals, loops, and functions. The students will work on mini-projects to practice these concepts. The program will end with a gallery walk so that everyone can share what they learned.
Lunch, snacks, t-shirts, and mini-prizes will be provided!
